Redefining the African Hope

$150 billion is spent annually on global aid, yet poverty still remains.

All that money. And still no relief. Something's not working. We believe the traditional relief model is the problem - increasing people's dependency on charity rather than freeing them from it.

Hoopo (formerly known as PRICE) is a social enterprise that transforms impoverished communities through the power of work. Our unique investment model creates jobs that break the reliance on handouts, while delivering a return to our supporters as well. We put your kindness where it counts.

Our Vision & Mission

We envision a world where every person has the opportunity to live a life of dignity and purpose. We believe that the best way to achieve this is by creating jobs that empower people to break the cycle of poverty.

Global Impact

We have created over 1,000 jobs in 5 countries, and we are just getting started.
